Creamy Kewpie
Ramen & Egg

Mayo ramen is a savoury Japanese instant noodle dish that combines the intense flavour of ramen with the richness and creamy texture of Kewpie mayo. It's been making waves on Tiktok recently, but you don't have to be famous to make it! (Recipe courtesy of Honest Food Talks).

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of your favourite instant noodles
  • 1 tbsp Kewpie Mayo
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 egg
  • Your favourite toppings

Instructions

  1. Make the tangy and rich sauce with the same ingredients you need for instant noodles. Start by mixing together in a bowl 1 raw egg, 1 tablespoon of kewpie mayo, one tablespoon of minced garlic (or more - taste depending!) and whichever spice pack comes included with your kit's noodles.
  2. bring 500 ml water to a boil and cook the packet of instant noodles in a separate bowl. Follow the instructions on the packaging to cook perfectly.
  3. Once cooked, carefully pour half of the boiled water used to cook the noodles into the bowl of sauce. Mix the sauce thoroughly until it becomes a consistent creamy broth.
  4. Add the noodles into the broth, and complete the dish with any toppings you wish. Serve your mayo ramen and eat while itâ€™s hot!
